HARRY POTTER BIRTHDAY PARTY IDEAS

Every potterhead loves a Harry Potter birthday party. So here are some ideas for you.

Before a party, you need a party hat. So here's one made by us, especially for you.

To assemble the hat:

  1. Take a printout of the template
  2. Cut along the dotted line
  3. Punch 2 holes on the black dots
  4. Fold the white area
  5. Stick the white area on the back of the other end
